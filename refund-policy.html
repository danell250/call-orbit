Refund Policy

At CallOrbit, we offer digital services including platform access and contact center numbers. Please review our refund policy carefully.

1. General Policy

All sales are generally non-refundable once services have been activated or numbers assigned.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • Duplicate purchase.
  • Incorrect charges due to technical error.
  • Cancellation before number activation (contact support immediately via Contact Page).

3. Refund Process

Submit a refund request via our Contact Page within 14 days of purchase. Our support team will review and notify you of the decision. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Activated numbers or services already used.
  • Any third-party fees incurred during purchase.
  • Change of mind after service activation.

5. Contact

For refund requests or inquiries, please contact our support via Contact Page.
