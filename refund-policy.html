Refund Policy

Our approach to refunds is simple, transparent, and customer-friendly.

At CallOrbit, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with our services. If you encounter any issues with our platform, please review the following refund terms:

1. Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refunds are available only for subscriptions or purchases made directly through CallOrbit.
  • Refunds must be requested within 14 days of the original purchase.
  • Partial usage of a service does not automatically qualify for a refund.

2. Non-Refundable Items

  • Custom setup fees.
  • Third-party services or integrations billed through CallOrbit.
  • Any promotional credits or discounts applied to a purchase.

3. How to Request a Refund

  • Contact our support team via the Contact Page.
  • Include your account details, purchase date, and reason for the refund.
  • Our team will review your request and respond within 3–5 business days.

4. Refund Process

  • Approved refunds will be issued via the original payment method.
  • It may take 5–10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

5. Changes to this Policy

CallOrbit reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page.
