About CallOrbit

Our mission is to streamline contact center management with dedicated dashboards and portals.

Our Story

CallOrbit was born from the idea that managing global contact centers shouldn’t be complex. We wanted dashboards that are intuitive, beautiful, and intelligent—so teams can focus on serving customers, not struggling with tools. From a small garage project to a platform used worldwide, our journey is about making communication seamless and empowering every agent.

Our Team

We are a group of engineers, designers, and operations specialists who live and breathe technology and customer success. Our team spans multiple continents, blending creativity with analytics, and every line of code, every design, is crafted to make your contact center shine.
