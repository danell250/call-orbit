About CallOrbit

Our mission is to streamline contact center management with dedicated dashboards and portals.

2019 — Founding

CallOrbit was born from the frustration of juggling multiple dashboards and portals. Our goal was to simplify contact center management.

2020 — Early Development

Built our first unified dashboard and received valuable feedback from early beta users.

2021 — Expansion

Added more features like number porting, analytics, and role-based portals to support growing teams worldwide.

Our Values

  • Customer-first philosophy: Every decision prioritizes our clients.
  • Transparency: Honest and clear communication in all we do.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving dashboards and tools.
  • Teamwork: Collaborative solutions that make management seamless.
