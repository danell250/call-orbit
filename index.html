What is CallOrbit?

CallOrbit is a complete contact center management platform that gives managers full control over numbers, teams, and analytics in one unified solution.

How does analytics work?

Live dashboards provide real-time metrics on agent performance, call volumes, response times, and more. Generate detailed reports to optimize your operations.

Can I provision numbers globally?

Yes, CallOrbit provides global number coverage in over 150 countries, including easy porting and management from a single dashboard.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide support through live chat and email. Sales and onboarding questions typically receive a response within 2-4 business hours, with 24/7 live-chat coverage for urgent technical issues.

Is my data secure with CallOrbit?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with all major security standards including GDPR and SOC 2.

How quickly can I get started?

You can be up and running in minutes. Simply sign up, choose your numbers, and start managing your contact center right away.

Do you offer integrations with other tools?

Yes, CallOrbit integrates seamlessly with popular CRM systems, helpdesk software, and business tools to streamline your workflow.

What's included in the free trial?

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all features with no credit card required. Test everything before making a commitment.
