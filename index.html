Frequently Asked Questions

What is CallOrbit and who is it for? +

CallOrbit is a complete contact center management platform designed for operations managers, team leads, and business owners who need to centralize, monitor, and optimize their voice and digital contact operations, especially those with global or remote teams.

How does the live analytics dashboard work? +

Our dashboard connects directly to your call queues and agent activity, providing real-time metrics (e.g., AHT, occupancy, queue length). It uses a modern WebSocket connection for instant updates, allowing you to view and act on data with zero delay.

Is CallOrbit compliant with global data privacy laws? +

Yes. CallOrbit is built with a focus on compliance, including GDPR and CCPA readiness. We provide tools for secure data handling, audit logging, and configurable data retention policies to meet your jurisdictional requirements.
